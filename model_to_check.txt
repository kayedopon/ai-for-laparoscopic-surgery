Models that answer "What is happening in this frame?":
	MedGemma
	LLaVA
	BLIP-2

Models that answer "Which step of surgery is this?":
	EndoNet
	TeCNO

Models that answer "What action is being performed over time?"
	SlowFast
	Inflated 3D ConvNet
	TimeSformer

There are some models that are good specifically for tool detection:
	YOLOv8
	CenterNet++

The workflow here might be:
Video
 ↓
Frame Sampling
 ↓
Tool Detection → YOLOv8
Scene Description → BLIP-2
Scene Understanding → SlowFast / TimeSformer
 ↓
Surgical Phase Prediction (EndoNet / TeCNO)
